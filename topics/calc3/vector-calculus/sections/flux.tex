\section{Flux}

  Given a surface and a vector field, a flux is a measure of how many fluid
  pass throug the surface per unit time

  \begin{align}
    \text{flux} &= \int \int_{S} \left( F \cdot \vec{n} \right) ds \\
    \vec{n} &=
    \begin{cases}
      r_{u} \times r_{v} \\
      r_{v} \times r_{u}
    \end{cases}
  \end{align}
